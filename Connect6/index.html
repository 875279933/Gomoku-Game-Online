🎯 What is Connect6 Game Online?

📖 How to Play This Connect6 Game

Playing Connect6 Game is straightforward but strategically rich: Black places 1 stone first, then both players take turns placing 2 stones per turn. The goal is to be the first to get six stones in a row – horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. Our Connect6 Online interface gives you full control: click any intersection to place your stone, then watch the AI respond with 2 stones. Use undo to learn from mistakes and reset to start a fresh challenge.

⚡ Why Connect6 is Fairer Than Gomoku

The genius of Connect6 lies in its "double-move" rule. After Black's first move, each player places 2 stones per turn. This ensures the stone count difference is always exactly 1, giving the second player (White) the ability to counterattack effectively. No complex forbidden moves are needed – the game is naturally balanced.

2. Why does Connect6 use 2 stones per turn after the first move?▼
The "double-move" rule is the key to Connect6's fairness. By allowing 2 stones per turn after the first move, the second player can always counterattack effectively, eliminating the huge first-move advantage that plagues traditional Gomoku.
3. Can I undo multiple moves while playing Connect6 Online?▼
Yes! Undo removes the last move (including AI's 2 stones). You can undo step by step back to an empty board. Perfect for learning the nuances of Connect6 strategy.
4. Is Connect6 more complex than Gomoku?▼
Yes, Connect6 has much higher complexity, similar to Go. The double-move rule creates exponentially more possible positions and requires deeper strategic thinking. This makes Connect6 a more challenging and rewarding game for experienced players.
5. What happens if the board fills up without a winner?▼
If the board is completely filled and no player has connected six stones, the game ends in a draw. This is rare but possible in well-matched games with strong defensive play.
