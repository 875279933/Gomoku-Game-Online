What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the website remember your actions or preferences (e.g., board theme, sound settings) over a period of time.

How we use cookies on Play Gomoku Online

We use only essential and functional cookies to make your Gomoku experience smooth. We do NOT use third-party advertising or tracking cookies unless we explicitly notify you.

Your cookie choices

You can control and/or delete cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block all cookies, but that may affect the functionality of our Gomoku game (for instance, your game progress may not be saved).

  • Google Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Mozilla Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data
