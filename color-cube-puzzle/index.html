The game

What is Color Cube Puzzle?

Color Cube Puzzle is a free browser logic game. A small board holds a stack of colored cubes. Underneath sit four conveyors, one for each color. Send every cube to the lane that matches its color. When the board empties, the level clears.

Each lane has a hard cap of three. Locked cubes are gated behind two same-color keys. Bomb cubes carry a shared fuse that ticks down on every wrong move. Run it out and the run ends. Beat the par to earn three stars.

There is no timer, no lives, no pop-up ads. You tap a cube, you tap a conveyor, the cube slides home. The design rewards planning over speed, which is why it lands well as a coffee-break puzzle on phones and a slow-evening one on the desktop.

This build is an original browser take on the same idea. The rules are simple, the levels get tighter, and the whole thing sits on a single page with no third-party scripts.

Getting started

How to play Color Cube Puzzle

Two taps clear a cube. The skill is in choosing the right order under the fuse clock.

  1. Read the board before the first tap. Note the lane fill, the locks, the bombs, and the color that runs out first.
  2. Tap a cube on the board. A white border marks your pick. Locked cubes are off-limits until unlocked.
  3. To unlock, send two same-color cubes first. The lock count drops each time a matching cube clears.
  4. Tap the matching conveyor. The cube slides home, the lane fill grows by one segment.
  5. Watch the fuse. A bomb is on the board. Every wrong-color send burns one fuse tick. The fuse ends the run.
  6. Defuse the bombs in the same pass as the rest of the cubes. Lane caps at three. Plan the order, not just the color.
  7. Beat the par score for three stars. Finish within par plus two for two. Clear the board to pass.

Strategy

Color Cube Puzzle tips

The rule is short. The skill sits in the order you tap, and in not wasting the fuse.

  • Read the board top to bottom before the first tap. Note which color runs out fastest and which lanes are close to cap.
  • Crack the locks first. The keys use the same color, so send the unlocked cubes of that color before any locked one.
  • Spot the bombs early. A bomb takes a slot in the lane just like any other cube, so plan it into the order.
  • Treat the fuse as a move budget. A wrong-color send burns one tick. Three or four wrong picks and the run ends.
  • Watch the lane capacity markers. A near-full lane means that color is close to its end. Plan the rest around the cap.
  • Stay close to par. The par score is set tight on purpose. One wrong move drops a star and burns a fuse tick.
  • When two colors share a count, sort the larger cluster first. The board stays cleaner for the bombs at the back.
  • Reset is free. If the order goes wrong, hit Reset Level and try a different sequence.
