🎯 What is Gomoku Game Online?

Gomoku Game Online, also known as "Five in a Row", is a classic abstract strategy game. In this digital version of the timeless Gomoku Game, two players alternate placing black and white stones on a 15×15 board. The first to form an unbroken line of five stones horizontally, vertically, or diagonally wins. Simple rules, deep tactics — a true brain sport. 📖 How to Play This Gomoku Game

Playing a Gomoku Game is straightforward: take turns placing your stones (black first). The goal is to be the first to get five stones in a row – horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. ⚡ Strategy Lab: Four-Three Attacks & Defending Live Threes

Mastering these tactics will elevate your Gomoku Game dramatically. In any serious Gomoku Online match, pattern recognition is key:

  • 🔹 Four-Three (fork): A forced win setup. Example: after placing a stone, you have one line with an open four (—OOOO—) and a separate line with a live three (—OOO— with both ends free). The opponent can block only one line, you play the winning move on the other, This is a hallmark of high-level Gomoku Game strategy.
  • 🔹 How to create a four-three: Look for “broken three” patterns or overlapping threats. Often a well-timed diagonal extension turns a simple three into a game-winning fork, Practice this in our Gomoku Online training mode.
  • 🔹 Defending a live three: The only safe way is to place a stone on one of the two open ends of the three. Playing elsewhere usually loses. On Hard difficulty, our AI always identifies and blocks live threes with 2‑ply lookahead, making this Gomoku Game a true tactical test.
  • 🔹 Pro tip: Use the “Undo” button to explore alternative defenses against AI’s live three threats — a great way to train pattern recognition for any Gomoku Game.

📌 These advanced tactics are used by professional Gomoku players. The AI on Hard difficulty simulates this strategic depth, offering one of the most engaging Gomoku Online experiences.
